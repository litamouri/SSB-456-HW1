Bali

Bali is one of the most evocative places to visit in the entire Indonesian archipelago.
A visit here sparks the senses. The intoxicating fragrance of incense and clove oil
hangs in the thick tropical air. Peanuts sizzle at roadside stalls, petal-strewn offerings
smolder on busy sidewalks, and traditional gamelan music jangles against the buzz of mopeds.©
